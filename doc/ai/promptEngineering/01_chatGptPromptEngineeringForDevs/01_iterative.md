# Iterative Prompt Development
- Prompting rarely works on the first attempt. Often times you need to follow a cycle of iterative improvements until you get the desired result.

## Cycle
1. Idea
    1. Be clear and specific
1. Implementation (code/data) Prompt
1. Experimental Result
1. Error Analysis
    1. Analyze why the result did not give the desired output
    1. Refine the idea and prompt
1. Repeat
