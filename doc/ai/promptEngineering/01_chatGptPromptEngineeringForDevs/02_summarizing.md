# Summarizing
One of AI's most powerful use cases is in summarizing. Summarizing provides the ability to have AI analyze the documents to provide you condensed content to be able to review more documents.
<br>
One of the more powerful ways that you can summarize is by providing additional prompts to target specific aspects of a document (aka by providing feedback on a specific department within a corporate report)
<br>
A different version of summarizing is extraction. You can prompt the AI to extract data from a report.
